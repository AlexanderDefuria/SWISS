A New Scouting Tool for the FIRST Robotics Competition

Introducing SWISS Scouting.

SWISS Scouting is a web application built by FRC Team MaxTech 4343 for scouting matches and creating strategies during an entire competition season.

Why use SWISS?

Cross-Platform

SWISS is a web-based application, which means you can use it wherever you want, on almost anything you want.

Multiple Users

Got a big scouting team? SWISS supports multiple users entering and accessing data at the same time. Gone are the days of paper forms and crowding around the lead scout's laptop!

No WiFi? No Problem!

WiFi hotspots aren't allowed at competitions. We've built a version of SWISS that runs off a Raspberry Pi - all you need is the Pi, a power source and your Bluetooth-enabled devices.
